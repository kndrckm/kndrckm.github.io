Modernist Library

Client Berlin City Council
Area 45,000 sq ft
Year 2022 — 2024
Status Completed

The Weight of Knowledge

The Modernist Library is an exercise in reduction. By utilizing raw board-formed concrete as both structure and finish, the building achieves a timeless permanence.

Every joint in the concrete, every shadow cast by the rhythmic facade, serves to remind the visitor of the tactile reality of space.
